# 06-11: Remaining Routes (Quick Reference)

## 06: Saffron City & Fuchsia City

### Saffron City
- Give drink to guard (from Celadon vending machines)
- Silph Co. (Team Rocket takeover)
- Defeat Giovanni (2nd time)
- Get Master Ball
- Saffron Gym: Sabrina (Psychic-type)
- Badge: Marsh Badge (6/8)

### Route 16-18 (Cycling Road)
- Need Bicycle
- Snorlax blocks Route 16 (use Poké Flute)
- Fast downhill path to Fuchsia

### Fuchsia City
- Safari Zone (500 steps, catch rare Pokémon)
- Get Surf HM (Safari Zone)
- Get Strength HM (Warden's house, need Gold Teeth from Safari Zone)
- Fuchsia Gym: Koga (Poison-type)
- Badge: Soul Badge (5/8)

**Key Pokémon:**
- Chansey (Safari Zone, rare)
- Kangaskhan (Safari Zone)
- Tauros (Safari Zone)
- Scyther/Pinsir (Safari Zone, version exclusive)
- Exeggcute (Safari Zone)

## 07: Seafoam Islands & Cinnabar Island

### Seafoam Islands
- Ice cave with puzzles
- Push boulders into holes
- Articuno (Legendary Ice/Flying-type) on B4F

### Cinnabar Island
- Pokémon Lab (revive fossils)
- Pokémon Mansion (find Secret Key)
- Cinnabar Gym: Blaine (Fire-type)
- Badge: Volcano Badge (7/8)

**Fossil Revival:**
- Helix Fossil → Omanyte
- Dome Fossil → Kabuto
- Old Amber → Aerodactyl

---

## 08: Power Plant & Viridian Gym

### Power Plant
- East of Route 10 (need Surf)
- Zapdos (Legendary Electric-type)
- Voltorb and Electrode (look like Poké Balls!)

### Viridian Gym
- Finally unlocked after 7 badges
- Gym Leader: Giovanni (Ground-type)
- Badge: Earth Badge (8/8)
- Can now access Victory Road

---

## 09: Victory Road & Elite Four

### Victory Road
- Cave connecting Route 23 to Indigo Plateau
- Need all 8 badges to enter
- Need Strength, Flash, Surf to navigate
- Moltres (Legendary Fire/Flying-type) inside

### Elite Four
1. Lorelei (Ice-type)
2. Bruno (Fighting-type)
3. Agatha (Ghost/Poison-type)
4. Lance (Dragon-type)
5. Champion Rival (Mixed team)

**Recommended Level:** 55-65

---

## 10: Post-Game

### Cerulean Cave
- Unlocked after defeating Elite Four
- Strongest wild Pokémon in game
- Mewtwo (Legendary Psychic-type) on B1F
- Level 70, very hard to catch

### Mew Glitch
- See `docs/glitches/mew-glitch.md`
- Requires specific setup
- Only way to get Mew

### Completing Pokédex
- Trade version exclusives
- Trade evolutions (Alakazam, Gengar, Machamp, Golem)
- Catch all legendaries
- Revive all fossils
- 151/151 complete!
